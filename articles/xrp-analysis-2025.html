Investment Strategy: Dollar-Cost Averaging

Don’t try to time the market perfectly! Instead, use dollar-cost averaging (DCA) to build your XRP position over time. Recommended DCA schedule: Weekly ($50-$200), Bi-weekly ($100-$400), Monthly ($200-$1000). DCA reduces the impact of volatility, removes emotion from investing, takes advantage of dips automatically, and builds discipline and consistency.

Portfolio Allocation Strategy

Conservative Approach: 5-10% of crypto portfolio. Perfect for investors new to crypto or those with lower risk tolerance.

Moderate Approach: 15-25% of crypto portfolio. Balanced exposure with significant upside potential.

Aggressive Approach: 30-50% of crypto portfolio. For investors who believe in XRP’s long-term potential and can handle volatility.

Risk Management: Never invest more than you can afford to lose. Diversify across multiple cryptocurrencies, set stop-losses if actively trading, take profits gradually on the way up, and keep your emergency fund separate from crypto investments.
